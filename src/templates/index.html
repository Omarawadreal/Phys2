Welcome to Physics 2!

This course aims to enable students to understand concepts and phenomena and solve problems in electricity and magnetism based on fundamental laws of physics and provide a basic understanding of electric circuits

We're excited to have you in the course and look forward to helping you develop a deep understanding in the scope of electrical Physics

Topics we will cover in this course:

Electric Fields, Gauss’s Law, Electric Potential, Capacitance and Dielectrics, Current and Resistance, Direct-Current Circuits, Magnetic Fields, Sources of Magnetic Fields, Faraday’s Law, Inductance

Note: please make sure to read the Syllabus for this course as you are responsible for doing so.
